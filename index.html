About me

I am a product engineer with two years of professional experience at Nabla where I learnt to grow and ship human-focused products for doctors and healthcare workers.

At Nabla I grew from a role of Natural Language Processing Engineer to full Sotfware Engineering and Product Leadership: from shipping state of the art NLP features and building ML tooling, to collecting user feedback and writing product specifications with a vision.

I've had the extraordinary chance to learn engineering and product development in a highly demanding environment that strived to implement the highest standards in terms of privacy and software quality.

I have successfully led small teams of engineers on small to bigger projects, onboarded new people and gave interviews. This is a role I strive in.

What can I do for you

I am interested in both hands-on engineering and management roles. Here are some of the things I can do for you.

Architect and implement modern, high-quality web applications and backend systems, combining good programming practices and latest tools and technologies.

Devise, implement, distribute and integrate NLP and ML algorithms at state-of-the-art performances into your products.

Build and lead small engineering teams with a focus on autonomy and self-fulfillment in a fun and sustainable work environment.

Technologies

I am proficient in web application design and engineering using React, GraphQL, TypeScript, and client-side state management with Apollo. I have also shipped several features in React Native, but albeit proficient, I am not an expert.

In parallel I am proficient in architecturing and maintaining performant back-end services, notably in Golang and Kotlin for GraphQL and ML microservices with gRPC.

At Facebook AI Research I have worked with the leading research team on conversational bots, where I helped explore transformer architecture and devised better automatic evaluation methods. At Nabla I have led the NLP effort and developped an in-house framework for research and deployment of focussed and integrated AI features. I have notably worked on ML Ops and delivered a self updating system for Named Entity Recognition in Kubernetes.

I am passionate about product building and building trusting relations with users and designers.

I have a solid grasp of software engineering fundamentals such as functional and object-oriented programming paradigms, relational database design, application and system architectures.

I am familiar with modern cloud deployment, hosting, and data storage strategies and architectures, though I am by no means an expert.
