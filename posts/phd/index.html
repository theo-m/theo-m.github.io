tldr: wrote a PhD proposition, did not do the PhD, the document is here

During the internship the PhD plan was growing more and more mature. So when it ended in the last days of August we moved on to write a formal thesis proposition.

That process was both exhilerating and stressful. Exhilerating because I basically made it into a shopping list of the topics I wanted to explore. Stressful because we were building a PhD between the Facebook AI labs and the Institut Jean-Nicod, a language philosophy lab at ENS, Paris. That meant that the proposition would be read by two very different groups of people, whose expectations would surely be disappointed - I was mainly afraid of reception from the language philosophy people since I did not receive a lot of education on these topics, despite my apetite for it.
