Joining Nabla

June 2, 2019

Met Alexandre Le Brun and Martin Raison during my internship at FAIR during summer ‘18. They left the office to start Nabla about a month after I’d arrived.

I’ve always had an interest in building things and looked for opportunities to be closer to the “real world”. So while I was writing my PhD proposal I started part-time there to be part of the adventure and try to help where I could.

Once the PhD prospects died out, I joined full time as Machine Learning engineer. During the first few months we iterated a bit with different customers but it became clear that this wasn’t what we wanted to do, so we set out to build something for ourselves. I think most of the frustration at the time came from not being responsible for the decision and the implementation of our product.

Hence the shift, and we’re now a healthcare company!
